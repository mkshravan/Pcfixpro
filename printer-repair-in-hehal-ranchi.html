Common Printer Problems We Fix in Hehal, Ranchi

  • Printer not printing: Caused by empty/clogged cartridges, print head blockage, driver issues, or queue errors. PCFIXPRO diagnoses and fixes the exact cause.
  • Paper jam: The most common printer issue. Caused by worn rollers, misfeed trays, or foreign objects. PCFIXPRO clears jams and replaces worn rollers.
  • Poor print quality: Faded, streaked, or blurry prints are caused by low ink, clogged print heads, or a worn drum. PCFIXPRO restores print quality to factory standard.
  • Printer not recognized by computer: Driver corruption, USB port issues, or firmware bugs. PCFIXPRO reinstalls drivers and fixes connectivity.
  • WiFi printing not working: Network configuration issues or firmware bugs prevent wireless printing. PCFIXPRO configures wireless printing for all devices.
  • Printer making noise: Grinding, clicking, or rattling sounds indicate worn gears, rollers, or carriage faults. PCFIXPRO replaces the damaged components.
  • Error codes and flashing lights: Error codes indicate specific hardware faults. PCFIXPRO interprets and resolves all manufacturer error codes.
  • Laser printer fuser issues: Fuser unit failure causes paper to come out with smeared or unfused toner. PCFIXPRO repairs or replaces fuser units.
