Our Story

From a One-Person
Workshop to Ranchi's
Most Trusted Tech Brand

PCFixPro.in was founded in 2012 by Rajan Kumar, a CompTIA A+ certified engineer who saw that Ranchi lacked a reliable, honest, and affordable computer repair service. Starting with a small workshop in Lalpur with just one technician and a toolkit, Rajan began solving the tech problems of students, home users and small businesses — one device at a time.

What set PCFixPro apart from day one was a simple commitment: no surprise charges, no unnecessary part replacements, and no work done without your approval. Word spread quickly. By 2015, PCFixPro had grown into a team of 6 technicians and expanded its services to include printer repair, data recovery and networking.

Today, PCFixPro.in is a 18-strong team of certified engineers serving homes, startups, corporates, hospitals and government offices across all of Ranchi and Jharkhand. With 15,000+ successful repairs and a 4.9-star Google rating, PCFixPro is the name Ranchi trusts when technology fails.
