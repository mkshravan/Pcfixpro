Common Computer Problems We Fix in Kanke, Ranchi

  • Computer not turning on: Could be a power supply fault, motherboard issue, or dead CMOS battery. PCFIXPRO diagnoses the exact cause chip-level.
  • Very slow PC: An old HDD, insufficient RAM, or virus infection slows computers dramatically. SSD upgrade or RAM addition resolves this immediately.
  • No display / blank screen: GPU failure, monitor cable issue, or motherboard fault. PCFIXPRO identifies and fixes the cause correctly.
  • Virus and malware infection: Ransomware, spyware, and trojans compromise your data and slow your PC. PCFIXPRO removes all threats and reinstalls Windows 10/11 fresh.

  • Blue Screen of Death (BSOD): Hardware faults or driver conflicts cause BSODs. PCFIXPRO diagnoses and fixes the underlying hardware or software fault.
  • USB or port not working: Faulty USB controllers or damaged ports repaired at component level.
  • Networking issues: WiFi card replacement, network configuration, and office LAN setup.
  • GPU / graphics card failure: No display, artifacting, or crashes from GPU. PCFIXPRO repairs at chip level or replaces the GPU.
